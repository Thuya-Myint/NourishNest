Name - AnnaWillis

I am a passionate Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN) with over 8 years of experience specializing in weight management and sports nutrition. Holding a Master’s degree in Nutritional Sciences from the University of Good Health, my career mission is to empower individuals to achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized nutrition planning and evidence-based dietary strategies.

Educational Credentials

  • MS in Nutritional Sciences, University of Good Health, 2016
  • BS in Dietetics, Health Science College, 2014

Licenses and Certifications:

  • Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN), Commission on Dietetic Registration, 2017
  • Licensed Dietitian Nutritionist (LDN), State of Wellbeing, 2017
  • Certified Specialist in Sports Dietetics (CSSD), 2019

Professional Experience

  • Senior Nutritionist, Healthy Living Wellness Center (2019-Present) Developed personalized nutrition plans for over 500 clients, focusing on sustainable weight loss, muscle gain, and improved athletic performance. Led nutrition workshops and seminars.
  • Clinical Dietitian, St. Good Health Hospital (2017-2019) Collaborated with healthcare teams to provide medical nutrition therapy for patients with diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, and other conditions. Improved patient satisfaction scores by 20%.

Publications and Research

  • Doe, J. (2022) “Nutritional Strategies for Endurance Athletes.” Journal of Sports Nutrition and Exercise Metabolism.
  • Contributed to “The Comprehensive Guide to Weight Management” (2020), a collaborative book by leading nutrition experts.

Professional Affiliations

  • Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, Member since 2016
  • Sports, Cardiovascular, and Wellness Nutrition (SCAN) dietetic practice group, Member since 2017
